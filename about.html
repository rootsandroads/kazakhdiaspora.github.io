A Note From Our Founder

My journey as a Kazakh student abroad inspired me to create a space where our stories don't get lost in statistics. Roots and Roads is a living map of the Kazakh diaspora – a place to see where we study, what we dream about, the challenges we face, and the ways we grow.

From personal essays to data-driven insights, this project aims to make our experiences visible: to future students, to universities, and to anyone curious about life beyond borders. I hope Roots and Roads becomes your go-to place for inspiration, honest reflections, and a reminder that you are not the only one carving a path far from home.

xo, Danelya

Danelya Aitmagambet

Founder, Roots and Roads – Kazakh Diaspora Project

Danelya is a Kazakh student studying abroad who wanted a place where numbers and maps meet real human stories. Between exams, visas, and late-night calls home, she built Roots and Roads to connect students, highlight success stories, and show the world how diverse and ambitious the Kazakh diaspora truly is.
